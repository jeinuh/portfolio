About Jane

I'm Jane, a senior at UCSD studying design and interaction hailing from the SF Bay Area. I've been finding joy in design since I was in middle school, customizing my blog pages by positioning web elements or implementing scripts to get posts to dynamically load. Currently, beyond formally learning as much as I can about conventional design paradigms and technical frameworks, I am interested in how thoughtful design be used towards environmental justice, accessibility, and racial equity.

When I'm not finetuning my design and webdev skills, you can also find me tending to my numerous houseplants or vegetable garden, watching old foreign movies, taking digital photos of natural landscapes, reading Japanese surrealist/crime novels, and/or enjoying a picnic.
